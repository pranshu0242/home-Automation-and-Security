About Us

The Hi-Tech world is currently growing and can bring a lot of opportunities for businesses, and IoT for smart homes is no exception. Many world-famous companies have already created great products and solutions. August smart lock has the reputation of the most reliable one, just like Nest’s thermostat is considered to be the best solution for adjusting the climate of one’s home. Smart home assistants are also in high demand right now. For example, Google Home or Amazon’s Alexa helps to manage smart devices reacting to your voice commands.

It is needless to say the importance of automation and security systems in the present era. That’s why we are trying to automate little such as turning the lights on or off by using just verbal commands or turn on or off a fun just by saying “Alexa turn off the fan” also we are making a smart electrical board which will turn on or off the devices on time set by the user so there is no wastage of electricity and a water leakage detector which will turn off the water motor when it detects the water tank is full.

We are also making some security modules also like a gas leakage detector which will notify you when it detects LPG gas leakage so you can take quicker action and an NFC door which will only open when using NFC tags. To do all this we are using Arduino and making 5 modules each will use different sensers which will help them to make a normal house turn into a smart home. IoT solutions for smart homes still remain expensive and not everyone can afford them. But as the prevalence grows, the cost slowly reduces. The industry is predicted to reach its top popularity in a decade. It means that you still have time to get prepared and hit the market with an IoT smart home solution that will be used by a wide audience.
